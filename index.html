💡 What are ROIs? Regions of Interest are specific face areas where we measure color changes caused by blood flow. Different areas have different signal quality because of skin thickness, blood vessel density, and motion artifacts.

📖 How to Use This App

  1. Position yourself in good, even lighting (avoid shadows on face)
  2. Click "Start Camera" and allow camera access
  3. Click "Show ROIs" to see colored regions on your face
  4. Stay still and watch the signals appear below
  5. Observe how SNR (Signal-to-Noise Ratio) differs between regions
  6. Notice that forehead and cheeks typically have the best signals!

💡 Pro tip: Forehead usually has high SNR because it has many blood vessels and minimal movement. Cheeks are good too, but can be affected by facial expressions!
